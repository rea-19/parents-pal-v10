How to earn your rewards! ▾

Below, you’ll see the option to select a goal. Find one that works best for you so that you can start gaining points now. Once you select your goal, the more events you attend, the more points you get. You can also gain points from leaving reviews on the events you have been to. Keep an eye on the progress bar to know when you have reached your goal!
