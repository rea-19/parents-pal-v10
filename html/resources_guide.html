ParentPal is your go-to platform for discovering exciting and family-friendly events tailored to children of all ages. Designed with busy parents in mind, ParentPal makes it easy to explore what’s happening in your local area with just a few clicks. Whether you’re searching for music classes, sports activities, educational workshops, or fun weekend outings, the platform helps you find events that match your child’s interests and your family’s schedule. One of ParentPal’s standout features is its smart filtering system, allowing you to customise your search by age group, location, event type, or time of day. You can also save your preferences, ensuring that relevant events appear first every time you log in. As you engage with the platform, you’ll even have the chance to collect rewards that can be used toward future events—helping families not only plan better but also save along the way.

Sign Up

Get bonus points instantly, plus extra when you refer friends. First time signed up would give you 15 points rewards!

Book

Earn points every time you book an event. Each event that's booked will give you 1 point or more. You can use the collected points as discounts for paid events!

Scan

Scan your card at events to collect points automatically. So, just by showing up to the events you are interested in joining, can give you more points!

Events are not booked on the ParentPal website. You can only browse available events on this website and you will be redirected to the official events' account and booked from there. Once you have booked the events you are interested with, you will receive a point from ParentPal!

You can save your filters by answering the quiz (when you signing in) and click on the save preferences button. All your preferences such as age allocated for the events, cost of the events, activity type, and prefered suburb will be saved, and you can apply your preferences anytime you are signed in. Only the date range will not be saved.

You can clicked on the profile page and hover on the My Events page and see your booked events and favourite events you saved, tailored with the nearest date to the latest date!
